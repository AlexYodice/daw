Instructions

  • Click on add instrument on the bottom left corner of the dashboard
  • Or you can just click on the record button (default instrument is piano)
  • Clicking on the piano keys or using the keyboard while recording will create visual sound-bytes in the dashboard
  • You can add more instruments and record new sounds as well, adding multiple instruments to your musical masterpiece!

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • "Space" - Play/Pause
  • "Shift" + "Space" - Beging Recording
  • "." - Increase Octave
  • "," - Decrease Octave
